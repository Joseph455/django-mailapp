Just getting started

If your new to M-sender here is a quick guide to get you going

M-sender is a light weight mail sending app which allows you to send mails to audience of any size with ease ans flexibility.
To send a mail in M-sender ist you need to:

  1. Create and populate a mailing list.
  2. Next you you add a carrier mail a carrier mail is an email account you use to send mails to your audience
  3. Once you have added your carrier mail you need to configure your carrier mail click to see how.
  4. Finally you go to the Mail section where you can compose and send emails to your audience.
